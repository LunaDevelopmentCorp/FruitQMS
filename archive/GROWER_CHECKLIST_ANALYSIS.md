# GLOBALG.A.P. Grower Checklist Analysis

## Purpose
This document analyzes the 191 GLOBALG.A.P. IFA v6.0 control points to determine which apply to:
1. **Central Management Only** - Applies to central organization/packhouse
2. **Grower-Specific** - Must be completed by each individual grower
3. **Both/Shared** - Can apply to both levels depending on operation type

## GFS vs SMART Audit Scope Context

### GFS (Individual Producer - Option 1)
- Single grower OR single packhouse
- One unified checklist for that entity
- All applicable control points in one location
- **Control points that apply**: All relevant to their operation type

### SMART (Multi-Site - Option 2)
- Central management system (policies, procedures, oversight)
- Multiple contract growers
- **Central organization has**: Management system control points
- **Each grower has**: Field operation control points
- **Common responses allowed**: Many growers may have same implementation

## Control Point Categories by Applicability

### 1. CENTRAL MANAGEMENT ONLY (26 points)
These apply only to the central organization, not individual growers.

#### FV 01 - Internal Documentation (4 points)
- FV-GFS 01.01 - Document control procedure
- FV-GFS 01.02 - Record retention
- FV-GFS 01.03 - Document accessibility
- FV-GFS 01.04 - Internal audits
**Rationale**: Central management system responsibility

#### FV 02 - Continuous Improvement Plan (2 points)
- FV-GFS 02.01 - Continuous improvement plan exists
- FV-GFS 02.02 - Implementation evidence
**Rationale**: Strategic planning at central level

#### FV 03 - Resource Management and Training (4 points)
- FV-GFS 03.01 - Roles and responsibilities defined
- FV-GFS 03.02 - Competence requirements
- FV-GFS 03.03 - Training records
- FV-GFS 03.04 - Training effectiveness
**Rationale**: Central HR and training coordination

#### FV 06 - Traceability (1 point)
- FV-GFS 06.01 - Traceability system
**Rationale**: Central system oversight, though growers provide data

#### FV 07 - Parallel Ownership (4 points)
- All parallel ownership control points
**Rationale**: Only applies to operations with multiple schemes

#### FV 09 - Recall and Withdrawal (1 point)
- FV-GFS 09.01 - Recall procedure
**Rationale**: Central management responsibility

#### FV 10 - Complaints (2 points)
- FV-GFS 10.01 - Complaint procedure
- FV-GFS 10.02 - Worker grievance mechanism
**Rationale**: Central policy and system

#### FV 14 - Food Safety Policy (1 point)
- FV-GFS 14.01 - Food safety policy
**Rationale**: Central policy document

#### FV 15 - Food Defense (1 point)
- FV-GFS 15.01 - Food defense assessment
**Rationale**: Central risk assessment

#### FV 16 - Food Fraud (1 point)
- FV-GFS 16.01 - Food fraud vulnerability assessment
**Rationale**: Central risk assessment

#### FV 17 - Logo Use (1 point)
- FV-GFS 17.01 - GLOBALG.A.P. logo usage
**Rationale**: Central compliance with trademark rules

#### FV 18 - GLOBALG.A.P. Status (1 point)
- FV-GFS 18.01 - Transaction documentation includes GGN
**Rationale**: Central commercial documentation

#### FV 33 - Postharvest Handling (12 points)
- All postharvest/packhouse operations
**Rationale**: Packhouse-specific operations (if packing included)

**SUBTOTAL: ~35-40 control points** (Central Management Only)

### 2. GROWER-SPECIFIC (Must be completed by each grower) (90+ points)

These control points relate to field-level operations and must be documented separately for each grower location.

#### FV 26 - Plant Propagation (5 points)
- All plant material sourcing and propagation
**Rationale**: Each grower sources their own planting material

#### FV 28 - Soil and Substrate Management (10 points)
- FV-GFS 28.01.01 - Soil assessment
- FV-GFS 28.01.02 - Soil erosion prevention
- FV-GFS 28.01.03 - Soil organic matter
- FV-GFS 28.01.04 - Soil structure and compaction
- FV-GFS 28.02.x - Substrate management
**Rationale**: Field-specific soil conditions and practices

#### FV 29 - Fertilizers and Biostimulants (14 points)
- All fertilizer application records
- Storage (may be grower-specific or central)
- Justification for use
- Application equipment
**Rationale**: Each grower has unique soil needs and application records

#### FV 30 - Water Management (18 points) ← LARGEST FOR GROWERS
- FV-GFS 30.01.x - Water source risk assessment
- FV-GFS 30.02.x - Irrigation water quality
- FV-GFS 30.03.x - Water use efficiency
- FV-GFS 30.04.x - Water conservation
- FV-GFS 30.05.x - Irrigation equipment
**Rationale**: Field-specific water sources, quality testing, irrigation practices

#### FV 31 - Integrated Pest Management (8 points)
- FV-GFS 31.01 - IPM plan
- FV-GFS 31.02 - Pest monitoring
- FV-GFS 31.03 - Biological control methods
- FV-GFS 31.04 - Cultural controls
**Rationale**: Field-specific pest pressure and IPM strategies

#### FV 32 - Plant Protection Products (35 points) ← LARGEST SECTION
- FV-GFS 32.01.x - PPP selection and justification
- FV-GFS 32.02.x - Application records (CRITICAL - must be per grower/field)
- FV-GFS 32.03.x - Equipment calibration and maintenance
- FV-GFS 32.04.x - Empty container disposal
- FV-GFS 32.05.x - PPP storage
- FV-GFS 32.06.x - Maximum Residue Levels (MRLs)
**Rationale**: Each grower has unique spray programs, application records are legal requirements

#### Harvest-Related Points from FV 33 (subset)
- Field harvest hygiene
- Harvest container cleanliness (field level)
- Personnel hygiene during harvest
**Rationale**: Grower-controlled harvest operations

**SUBTOTAL: ~90 control points** (Grower-Specific)

---

### 3. BOTH/SHARED (Can apply to central or grower depending on setup) (60+ points)

These may have both central policies AND grower-level implementation records.

#### FV 19 - Hygiene (8 points)
- Can be central policy + grower implementation
- Toilet facilities, handwashing stations, etc.
**Applicability**: Central policy, grower compliance at field level

#### FV 20 - Workers' Health, Safety, and Welfare (15 points)
- Central policies on safety, risk assessments
- Grower-level training records, accident reports
**Applicability**: Central policies, grower-specific records

#### FV 21 - Site Management (6 points)
- Site history, risk assessments
**Applicability**: Each grower provides their own site information

#### FV 22 - Biodiversity and Habitats (7 points)
- Conservation areas, ecological focus areas
**Applicability**: Field-specific, each grower's land

#### FV 23 - Energy Efficiency (4 points)
- Energy monitoring and improvement plan
**Applicability**: Central for packhouse, grower-level if significant energy use

#### FV 25 - Waste Management (9 points)
- Waste disposal procedures
**Applicability**: Central policy, grower-level implementation

**SUBTOTAL: ~49 control points** (Both/Shared)

## Grower Checklist Scope for SMART (Option 2)

### Mandatory for Each Grower (90 points):
1. **FV 26** - Plant Propagation (5 points)
2. **FV 28** - Soil Management (10 points)
3. **FV 29** - Fertilizers (14 points)
4. **FV 30** - Water Management (18 points) ← Critical
5. **FV 31** - IPM (8 points)
6. **FV 32** - PPP Application Records (35 points) ← Critical

### Likely Common Responses Across Growers:
- **FV 26** - If all growers use same nursery/supplier
- **FV 29.02** - If central fertilizer storage facility
- **FV 32.05** - If central PPP storage (though application records differ)

### Individual Responses Required Per Grower:
- **FV 28** - Soil assessments (field-specific)
- **FV 29.01** - Fertilizer application records
- **FV 30.01-30.02** - Water source testing
- **FV 30.04** - Irrigation records
- **FV 31.02** - Pest monitoring records
- **FV 32.02** - Spray application records (MUST be individual)

## CSV Template Design

### Columns:
1. **Grower_Code** - Unique identifier (e.g., GR001, GR002)
2. **Grower_Name** - Farm name
3. **Control_Point_Code** - e.g., FV-GFS 30.02.01
4. **Control_Point_Section** - e.g., "FV 30 Water Management"
5. **Compliance_Status** - Compliant / Non-compliant / N/A
6. **Evidence_Filename** - Document reference
7. **Implementation_Notes** - How the grower meets this requirement
8. **Common_Response** - YES/NO - Can this response be duplicated to other growers?
9. **Last_Updated** - Date of last update

### Example CSV Format:
```csv
Grower_Code,Grower_Name,Control_Point_Code,Control_Point_Section,Compliance_Status,Evidence_Filename,Implementation_Notes,Common_Response,Last_Updated
GR001,Smith Orchards,FV-GFS 26.01,Plant Propagation,Compliant,supplier_cert.pdf,All plants from certified nursery ABC,YES,2026-01-15
GR001,Smith Orchards,FV-GFS 28.01.01,Soil Management,Compliant,soil_test_2025.pdf,Annual soil test conducted,NO,2026-01-15
GR001,Smith Orchards,FV-GFS 32.02.01,PPP Application,Compliant,spray_diary_2025.pdf,Complete spray diary maintained,NO,2026-01-20
GR002,Jones Farm,FV-GFS 26.01,Plant Propagation,Compliant,supplier_cert.pdf,All plants from certified nursery ABC,YES,2026-01-15
GR002,Jones Farm,FV-GFS 28.01.01,Soil Management,Compliant,soil_test_2025_jones.pdf,Annual soil test conducted,NO,2026-01-18
```

### CSV Functionality:
1. **Download Template**: Pre-populated with all grower-applicable control points
2. **Add Multiple Growers**: List same control points for multiple growers
3. **Common Responses**: Mark "YES" to indicate response can be duplicated
4. **Individual Responses**: Mark "NO" for grower-specific evidence
5. **Upload CSV**: Bulk import creates individual grower checklists
6. **Individual Updates**: After import, can update each grower checklist separately

## Summary

**Total GLOBALG.A.P. Control Points**: 191

**Distribution**:
- **Central Management Only**: ~35-40 points (19-21%)
- **Grower-Specific**: ~90 points (47%)
- **Both/Shared**: ~60 points (31%)

**For SMART Multi-Site Operations**:
- Central organization: ~40-100 points (depending on shared responsibilities)
- Each grower: ~90 points (mandatory field operations)
- Common responses possible: ~20-30 points (plant sourcing, some storage)
- Individual responses required: ~60-70 points (soil, water, applications)

**Critical Grower-Specific Sections**:
1. FV 32 - Plant Protection Products (35 points) - Application records
2. FV 30 - Water Management (18 points) - Water testing and irrigation
3. FV 29 - Fertilizers (14 points) - Application records
4. FV 28 - Soil Management (10 points) - Soil assessments
